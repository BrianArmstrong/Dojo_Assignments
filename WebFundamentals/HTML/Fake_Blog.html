Seattle Mariners Ultimate Player

In my mind, Ken Griffey Jr. is the best player to ever wear a Seattle Mariners uniform. He has won numerous Gold Gloves, Silver Slugger awards, an MVP award in 1997, and was elected to the Baseball Hall of Fame. No other player has had the type of impact on Seattle Mariners baseball, as well as MLB as a whole than him.
